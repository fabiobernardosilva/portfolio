V. STUDIO

Branding project created for the SS20 edition of Helsinki Fashion Week. Deliverables included large format designs for the event venues, the official posters, programme, brochure, and passes, as well as the event's online presence.

A highly scalable grid system was developed to accomodate content across all outputs. A variation of the core grid graphic was produced and provided to each of the 20 participant fashion designers. The neon colour was inspired by ecoLogicStudio's algae bio-curtain hung outside the main venue of the event.

Interior Design Project: Thutty Dezidério.
