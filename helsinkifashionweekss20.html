Helsinki Fashion Week SS20

Visual identity system created for the SS20 edition of Helsinki Fashion Week. Deliverables included large format designs for the venues of the event, the official posters, programme, brochure, and passes, as well as the event's online presence.

A highly scalable grid system was developed to accomodate content across all outputs. A variation of the core grid graphic was produced and provided to each of the 20 participant fashion designers. The neon colour was inspired by ecoLogicStudio's algae bio-curtain hung outside the main venue of the event.

Designed in collaboration with Jamie Smail.

Street Style Photography: Chau Hoang and Søren Jepsen.
